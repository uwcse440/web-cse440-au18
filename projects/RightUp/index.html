Why RightUp?

Posture plays an integral role in the healthy physical and mental development of a child. However, reminders and strict discipline tend to cause more harm than good, in fact creating anxiety in the child.

RightUp is a virtual reality platform that engages your children, teaching and having them model good posture. The system records your child's data and gives you in-depth diagnostics on how they and their posture are doing. From there, the system sends you additional information and instructionals on any concerns regarding your child's development, as well as any corrections you can help make.

Features

In-depth Diagnostics

Learning about posture development is easy in RightUp! The game delivers accurate diagnostics and information about your child's and your healthy development. VR camera technology senses the player's posture in real time, and analyze it against the standards suggested by medical experts.

RightUp also provides you with all you need to learn about solving those posture problems! No time to learn while playing? Information such as back/neck issues, mental health status, and further information and exercises can all be stored or sent to your phone for future viewing.

Explorations and Adventures

Explore an endless world in real time as a parent-child duo! You can follow the tasks that the game provides, and enjoy the rewards for completing the tasks. You are also free to explore on your own for an adventure into the wild.

When playing RightUp, the taller you stand the more you can see and interact with the world around you! Children unconsciously learn the benefits of good posture by exploring the world through the game.

Battles with Monsters

Fight to save the world of RightUp from hordes of evil posture monsters! Enjoy mysterious treasures as rewards for a brave fighter.

When you are in a battle, the better your posture is, the more powerful you are. What's more memorable for children than an exciting battle agaist the evil monsters? RightUp helps children learn the benifit of good posture, in the fun way.

Activities and Exercises

Play a range of activities and exercises that help you stretch out your body, relax your muscles and teach you the importance of good posture.
